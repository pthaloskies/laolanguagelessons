Introductions & Locating People

ENGLISHLAO SCRIPTPRONUNCIATIONWHEN / WHY
Who are you looking for? ມາຫາໃຜ? maa hăa phái? Lit: "Come to find who?"
I'm looking for ___ ຂ້ອຍມາຫາ ___ khōi maa hăa ___ Lit: "I come to find ___"
Is ___ here? ___ ຢູ່ບໍ່? ___ yuu bɔ̌ɔ? Asking for presence
Yes, is here. ຢູ່ yuu Answer: "Yes, here."
No, not here. ບໍ່ຢູ່ bɔ̌ɔ yuu Answer: "No, not here."
Please wait a moment. ຖ້າຈັກໜ້ອຍເດີ້ thâa cǎk nɔɔi dǝ̂ǝ Polite request
He/she will be here shortly. ຈັກໜ້ອຍລາວຊິມາ cǎk nɔɔi láo si mâa Lit: "A moment, he/she will come."

No, not long. ບໍ່ດົນ bɔ̌ɔ dôn Answer to "... a long time?"
How many years have you been in Laos? ເຈົ້າຢູ່ລາວ_ ໄດ້ຈັກປີແລ້ວ? câo yuu láo dâi cák pii lέεo? Follow-up question
